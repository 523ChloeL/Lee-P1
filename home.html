- ABOUT ME -

Crochet Crew is a club that strives to teach curious high school students the fiber art of crocheting. With the personal and social benefits that come with crocheting, we also have a goal to aid our local community. Volunteer team projects include crocheting collaborative blankets, amigurumi toys, and accessories for local animal shelters.
